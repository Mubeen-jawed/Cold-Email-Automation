How it works

Three questions. Then
it runs itself.

No spreadsheets. No copywriting. No follow-up reminders. Just set your target and let LeadFlow work through the night.

Step 01
🎯

Tell us who you want

Type the kind of business, pick a city, and describe what you offer. That's it. The whole setup takes under 60 seconds.

Step 02
🗺️

We find the leads

LeadFlow scans Google Maps and pulls hundreds of matching businesses — name, phone, website, ratings — all in minutes.

Step 03
🧠

AI qualifies the best ones

Every website is analyzed for weak spots — slow load times, missing contact forms, poor SEO. Only hot prospects get flagged.

Step 04
✉️

Real emails get discovered

LeadFlow visits each site and extracts actual contact addresses — no guessing games, no bloated bounce-prone lists.

Step 05
🚀

Personalized emails go out

Each email references the business by name, mentions their specific problem, and pitches your offer. Sent from your Gmail on your schedule.
